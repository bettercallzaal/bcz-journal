# For artists

You make music. The labels take the data, the platforms take the dollars, the algorithms take the attention. The ZAO is the alternative I'm building — a community where independent musicians own their work, their audience, and the system itself.

## Three things you can do, in order

### 1. Show up to a Fractal _(this week)_

The **ZAO Fractal** is our weekly community gathering. Members compare what they're building, give each other peer-reviewed Respect, and earn a spot in the rankings. It's how the ZAO governs itself and how new people earn standing. **6 PM EST, weekly.**

→ Join via [discord.thezao.com](https://discord.thezao.com) — ask in #general for the Fractal link.

You don't need to bring anything. Just show up. The Respect you earn is **soulbound** — it's tied to you, not for sale, and it's how the community measures who's actually doing the work.

### 2. Get featured on Farcaster `/zao` _(this week)_

Cross-post your latest release into the Farcaster `/zao` channel. The community curates: best work surfaces. Tracks that get traction get spotlighted in the [daily newsletter](https://paragraph.com/@thezao) (which has been published every day since January 1, 2025 and goes to a real, growing audience).

→ Get a Farcaster account at [farcaster.xyz](https://farcaster.xyz) → join `/zao` channel → cast.

### 3. Compete in WaveWarZ _(when you're ready)_

[WaveWarZ](https://www.wavewarz.com) is onchain music battles on Solana. Two artists, head-to-head, fans trade SOL on the outcome. Live every weeknight at **8:30 PM EST on YouTube**. Artists earn from the trading volume of their own battles — direct payout, no middleman.

→ Watch a few first. Reach out to [@WaveWarZ](https://x.com/WaveWarZ) on X when you have battle-ready tracks.

## What you actually get out of this

Three things, plainly:

- **A real audience for your music** — the daily newsletter, the Farcaster feed, the live battles. Your work gets in front of fans who actually pay attention.
- **Respect (the soulbound kind)** — over time, your contributions accumulate as on-chain reputation. It's recognized within the community and increasingly outside it.
- **A network of other independent artists** doing the same thing. People like [Jadyn Violet](https://www.thezao.com/community/jadyn-violet), [AttaBotty](https://www.attabotty.com), Clejan, Maxwell Aden, Jango UU, NessytheRilla, Songs of Eden. People you can collaborate with, learn from, and ship with.

## What this is NOT

- It's not a label. We don't sign you, take royalties, or own your masters.
- It's not a token sale. There's no "buy your way in." Membership is earned.
- It's not passive. If you don't show up, you don't earn Respect.
