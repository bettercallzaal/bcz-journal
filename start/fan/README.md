# For fans

You like music. You're curious about web3 because you've seen a lot of artists you follow start talking about it. You're not sure what's worth your attention. This page is what's worth your attention from this project specifically.

## Three things you can do, in order

### 1. Watch a WaveWarZ battle _(weeknight, 8:30 PM EST)_

Two artists go head to head. The community trades on the outcome (in SOL, on Solana). Songs get played in full. Whoever's working in the chat usually picks the matchups based on who's been hyping in `/zao`.

You don't need to trade or own anything to watch. It's just live music with stakes.

→ [wavewarz.com](https://www.wavewarz.com) for upcoming battles · [wavewarz.info](https://wavewarz.info) for the live stats dashboard

### 2. Subscribe to "Year of the ZAO"

Daily newsletter on Paragraph. Every single day since January 1, 2025. **Day 494 today** — never missed one. Mostly short — a thought, a happening, a milestone. It's the easiest way to follow what's actually going on in the community without scrolling Farcaster.

→ [paragraph.com/@thezao](https://paragraph.com/@thezao)

### 3. Find an artist to actually listen to

The ZAO has a roster of working independent musicians. Some are big, some are small. None are signed to traditional labels — all are publishing on their own terms.

Worth a click:

- **[Clejan](https://www.thezao.com/community)** — Atlanta. Rapper / classically trained violinist. Opened for Snoop Dogg, Busta Rhymes, Lindsey Stirling.
- **[Maxwell Aden](https://www.thezao.com/artists/maxwell-aden)** — discovered by RAab Stevenson (Justin Timberlake / Rihanna / SZA producer). 10M+ plays.
- **[Jadyn Violet](https://www.thezao.com/community/jadyn-violet)** — NJ. Started doing 24-hour livestream concerts on Twitter Spaces before everyone else realized you could.
- **[AttaBotty](https://www.attabotty.com)** — FL. Audiovisual artist. Makes some of the most interesting live-show animations in this space.
- **[Jango UU](https://www.thezao.com/community)** — NYC synthrock. Ships music as NFTs natively.

Full roster: [thezao.com/community](https://www.thezao.com/community)

## What you actually get

If you stick around, three things start happening:

- **You stop being just a listener.** Live battles, daily newsletter, Discord voice rooms — you can be in the same room as the artists making the work. Most of them will say hi.
- **You find better music.** This community curates aggressively. The bar to be on the [music releases page](https://www.thezao.com/music-releases) is real.
- **You learn how the new music economy works.** If web3 / onchain music is going to matter, this is one of the ground-floor spots to watch it happen in real time.

## What this is NOT

- It's not a streaming platform. We don't host the audio. Use Spotify / Apple / Sound.xyz / wherever the artist puts their work.
- It's not a Discord server full of NFT shills. Tone is different — show up and you'll see.
- It's not free of effort. To go from passive listener to part of the community, you have to actually show up. But the entry cost is just attention.
