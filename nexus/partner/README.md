# For press, partners, and prospective collaborators

The short version, then where to go.

## One paragraph

The ZAO is an impact network for independent musicians on Farcaster + Base. WaveWarZ is its onchain music battle product on Solana. ZABAL is the streaming and coordination layer that connects them. Built and operated by [Zaal Panthaki](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zaalp), an automation engineer at The Jackson Laboratory who started building this in 2022 and has shipped daily ever since. It's funded out of pocket and through service work; it has no token sale and no investors. Active community of 188+ on-chain Respect holders, ~1,000+ lifetime touchpoints, two annual festivals (NFT NYC, Art Basel Miami), and a third planned in Maine for 2026.
