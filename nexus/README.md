## The pillars

### 🎵 The ZAO — the impact network

Decentralized hub for independent musicians, artists, and technologists. Members earn **soulbound Respect** for showing up — running events, recording with each other, making the community work. No token sale, no "buy your way in." Build to belong.

- **Site:** [thezao.com](https://www.thezao.com)
- **Discord:** [discord.thezao.com](https://discord.thezao.com)
- **Farcaster channel:** [`/zao`](https://farcaster.xyz/~/channel/zao)
- **X:** [@thezaodao](https://x.com/thezaodao)
- **Festivals (IG):** [@zaofestivals](https://instagram.com/zaofestivals)
- **Daily newsletter:** [Year of the ZAO on Paragraph](https://paragraph.com/@thezao) · daily since Jan 1, 2025

### 🌊 WaveWarZ — onchain music battles

Solana-native music battles. Artists go head-to-head, fans trade ephemeral SOL-denominated tokens on outcomes. Live every weeknight at **8:30 PM EST on YouTube**.

- **Site:** [wavewarz.com](https://www.wavewarz.com)
- **Live stats:** [wavewarz.info](https://wavewarz.info)
- **X:** [@WaveWarZ](https://x.com/WaveWarZ)
- **Mobile app:** [wavewarzapp on GitHub](https://github.com/bettercallzaal/wavewarzapp) — Expo, alerts + spectator
- **Co-founder:** Ikechi Nwachukwu

### ⚡ ZABAL — the new front-end

The streaming + coordination layer that sits across The ZAO, WaveWarZ, and partner products. Token launches **January 1, 2026**.

- **Most recent update:** [ZABAL Update #3 (Dec 2, 2025)](https://paragraph.com/@thezao/zabal-update-3)
- **How it works (today):** SongJam runs the **$ZABAL leaderboard**. Connect wallet to participate. Stake **$SANG** for a sqrt multiplier on your ZABAL score.
- **Long-term vision:** **ZAO Stock 2026** — physical event in Maine

### 🧪 ZAOOS — the lab

The monorepo where everything new gets prototyped. When something is ready to be a product, it graduates: own repo, own database, own domain, code stripped from ZAOOS so there's no drift. Research stays in ZAOOS forever — it's the institutional memory.

- **Repo:** [github.com/bettercallzaal/zaoos](https://github.com/bettercallzaal/zaoos)
- **App:** [zaoos.com](https://zaoos.com)
- **Research library:** [202 docs across 13 topic folders](../research/) (music, governance, agents, infrastructure, cross-platform, more)
- **Fork it:** [FORK.md](https://github.com/bettercallzaal/zaoos/blob/main/FORK.md) — change [community.config.ts](https://github.com/bettercallzaal/zaoos/blob/main/community.config.ts) and you have your own community hub

## Choose your door

### I'm an artist

You probably want one of three things:

1. **Join the community.** Start at [thezao.com](https://www.thezao.com) → "Join the ZAO." Then show up to a [Fractal](https://paragraph.com/@thezao/year-of-the-zao-day-13) (weekly, 6 PM EST). Earning Respect is how you become a member that matters.
2. **Compete in WaveWarZ.** Battles are live every weeknight at 8:30 PM EST. Watch a few first; reach out via [@WaveWarZ on X](https://x.com/WaveWarZ) to be considered for the bracket.
3. **Get featured.** Cross-post your work on Farcaster `/zao`. Real engagement from the community → spotlight in the [daily newsletter](https://paragraph.com/@thezao).

### I'm a builder

You probably want to:

1. **Read the research.** [research/](../research/) — 200+ docs covering music infra, governance, Farcaster Mini Apps, cross-platform publishing, AI agents.
2. **Browse the project index.** [projects/](../projects/) — 103 repos clustered by theme, with the NEXUS lineage so the version-stack pattern actually makes sense.
3. **Fork ZAOOS.** Whole infrastructure for a gated Farcaster community. [FORK.md](https://github.com/bettercallzaal/zaoos/blob/main/FORK.md) gets you running locally in under an hour.
4. **Hire the connector.** [calendly.com/zaalp99/30minmeeting](https://calendly.com/zaalp99/30minmeeting).

### I'm a fan

You probably want to:

1. **Watch a battle.** [WaveWarZ on YouTube](https://www.wavewarz.com), every weeknight at 8:30 PM EST.
2. **Read the journal.** [bcz-journal](https://bettercallzaal.github.io/bcz-journal/) — what I'm working on, dated.
3. **Subscribe to the newsletter.** [paragraph.com/@thezao](https://paragraph.com/@thezao) — daily since Jan 1, 2025 and unbroken.

### I'm a partner or press

The shortest path: [calendly.com/zaalp99/30minmeeting](https://calendly.com/zaalp99/30minmeeting) or zaalp99 [at] gmail.
